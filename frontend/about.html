About Us

Sasula is a platform that allows you to sell your NFTs and charge per view to third parties. Our goal is to make it easy and accessible for creators to monetize their work through the power of blockchain technology.

At Sasula, we are committed to providing a seamless and user-friendly experience for our users. We believe in the power of decentralized finance and the limitless possibilities it can bring to the world of art and creativity. Join us today and discover the extraordinary world of NFTs!
